Usually we are asked this questions about Atomic Email Hunter:

How to start the search correctly?

The Mailbox plugin is excellent at extracting addresses from a user’s current email account. So, if you use this plugin, Atomic Email Hunter will scan all the email messages in the mailbox and extract the email addresses from Gmail, Hotmail, or any other mail service. You set the hunting parameters: You can search only in headers or scan email content, too.


  • The Yelp plugin searches emails on www.yelp.com and lets you use Atomic Email Hunter as a Yelp email scraper. All you need to do is enter the search options you want to use.

  • The Facebook plugin collects contact information of Facebook page owners (emails, names, job, website link, etc). Moreover, you set up the search criteria, so you will get a list of your target audience and potential clients data.

  • The Twitter plugin extracts emails from twitter.com and allows you to specify keywords, country, state, city and job title as a search data.
